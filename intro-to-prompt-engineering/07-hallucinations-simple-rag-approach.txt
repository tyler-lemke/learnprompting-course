What is the tool in edge that lets you check your z-indexs on the page?


Completion:

The tool in Microsoft Edge that allows you to check the **z-index** on a webpage is the **3D View tool**. This tool translates the web page into a 3D perspective, enabling you to explore and debug your webpage by navigating through the **DOM** and the **z-index stacking context**.

Here's the relevant information:

- **Tool:** 3D View tool
- **Purpose:** Explore the web page translated into a 3D perspective. Debug your webpage by navigating through the **DOM** or **z-index stacking context**.
